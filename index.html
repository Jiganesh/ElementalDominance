Welcome to the ELEMENTAL DOMINANCE.

The goal of the game is to pick the most powerful element than Evil Wizard.

You, the mighty wizard saviour of OZ will be playing against Evil Wizard who terrorizes a peacful village.

Power Cycle Rules :

Elements are arranged in life-cycle form, It is representation of the game which shows the power of element against another element.

For any element in Elemental Cycle:

- The element ahead and the element behind are the support element. If your opponent throws any of those elements you will be awarded a point.

-The element opposite are destruction or depleting element of choosen element. If your opponent throws any of those elements you will lose a point.

For same element you will be awarded a point as it seems you are stronger than Evil Wizard hence your element will do more damage.

For example, If you choose element "Fire" & Evil Wizard throws Wind or Lightning element then you will be awarded a point as those elements increase power of your "Fire" element.
